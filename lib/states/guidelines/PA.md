# Pennsylvania — High School NIL Guidelines

**Status:** Permitted with restrictions (72-hour disclosure + three-strikes ladder)
**Last verified:** 2026-04-29
**Primary source:** PIAA Handbook — Name, Image and Likeness Policy — [link](https://www.piaa.org/resources/handbook/default.aspx)

## Overview

The Pennsylvania Interscholastic Athletic Association (PIAA) Board of Directors approved its NIL policy in December 2022. PIAA's framework has two distinguishing features: a **72-hour disclosure window** to your principal or athletic director after entering an agreement, and a published **three-strikes ineligibility ladder** for repeat violations. PIAA also lists explicit banned product and service categories.

## Who can participate

Any PIAA-eligible student-athlete at a member school can pursue NIL. Eligibility is preserved as long as each deal complies with the rules below and is disclosed within 72 hours.

## Before you sign a deal

- Reread the contract and remove every reference to PIAA, your school, your team, or any school-identifying apparel.
- Confirm the brand and product category are not on PIAA's banned list (see below).
- Confirm no coach, school employee, or booster set up or facilitated the deal.
- If you are under 18, have your parent or legal guardian review and co-sign.

## When you sign a deal — your obligations

The 72-hour clock starts the moment you sign.

1. **Sign off school property** and outside team activity.
2. **Within 72 hours, disclose the agreement** to your **principal or athletic director**. Hand-deliver, or send from your own (or your guardian's) email — keep a timestamped copy.
3. **Include in your disclosure** a complete signed copy of the agreement and any exhibits.
4. **Save the school's acknowledgment** — written reply, email, or signed receipt.
5. **Do not wear school uniforms or "school-identifying apparel"** in any NIL content.
6. **Do not reference PIAA, your school, or your team name** in commercial content, captions, or scripts.
7. **Do not promote the deal during a school activity** or with school employees, coaches, or boosters involved.
8. **Re-disclose any amendment** within 72 hours of signing.

## Banned deal categories

PIAA explicitly prohibits NIL agreements promoting:

- **Adult entertainment** products and services
- **Alcohol** products
- **Casinos, gambling, and sports betting** (including online and game-based wagering)
- **Tobacco, e-cigarettes, and electronic smoking products**
- **Opioids and prescription pharmaceuticals**
- **Controlled substances**
- **Weapons, firearms, and ammunition**

You also may not enter deals that are conditioned on athletic performance, that use school marks/uniforms, or that are arranged by school employees or boosters.

## Penalties for non-compliance

PIAA uses a **three-strikes ladder**:

1. **First violation** — period of ineligibility (typically a temporary loss of eligibility)
2. **Second violation** — mandatory **one-year loss of PIAA eligibility**
3. **Third violation** — **permanent loss of PIAA eligibility** with no option for reinstatement

Failure to disclose within the 72-hour window is itself a violation. Treat the deadline as hard.
