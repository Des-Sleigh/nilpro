# Georgia — High School NIL Guidelines

**Status:** Permitted with restrictions
**Last verified:** 2026-04-29
**Primary source:** GHSA Constitution, Appendix N — Guidelines Regarding Name, Image and Likeness — [link](https://www.ghsa.net/constitution-section-2025-2026-appendix-n-guidelines-regarding-name-image-and-likeness)

## Overview

Georgia high school athletes can sign NIL deals as long as you keep the deal completely separate from your school. The Georgia High School Association (GHSA) governs this through Appendix N of its 2025-2026 Constitution. The rule is built around two ideas: you must remain an amateur under By-law 1.92-c, and the deal cannot be used to recruit you or keep you at a school under By-law 1.70.

## Who can participate

Any GHSA student-athlete enrolled at a member school, regardless of grade or sport, can do NIL deals. There is no minimum age or class-year requirement. The deal must be made by you as an individual — not as part of a team, not through your school, and not through a booster organization.

## Before you sign a deal

- Get your parent or guardian to read the contract with you. They will need to co-sign anything you sign as a minor.
- Confirm the deal is not contingent on athletic performance (touchdowns, goals, wins, stats). Performance-based pay violates GHSA amateurism.
- Confirm the brand is not affiliated with — or being routed through — your school, a coach, a booster club, or a "collective."
- Strip out any reference to your school, team, mascot, uniform, or GHSA logos in the deliverables.

## When you sign a deal — your obligations

1. **Disclose to your Principal or Athletic Director within 7 calendar days.** Appendix N requires you (or your parent/guardian) to notify the school in writing within 7 calendar days of entering the agreement. Send the email yourself.
2. **Get parent/guardian signature.** Minors cannot bind themselves to a contract in Georgia without a parent/guardian co-signature.
3. **Strip school marks from every deliverable.** No school logos, school name, school uniform, mascot, or trademarked GHSA logos in your photos, videos, or posts.
4. **Don't use school facilities.** No filming on the field, in the weight room, in locker rooms, or anywhere on campus for the deal.
5. **Keep records.** Save the contract, payment receipts, every social post, and your disclosure email for at least the duration of your high school career.

## Banned deal categories

- **Collectives and NIL Clubs.** Strictly prohibited. A "Collective" is any group organized to compensate or benefit student-athletes; an "NIL Club" is athletes pooling fans/funds. Joining or taking money from either ends your eligibility.
- **Tobacco, alcohol, controlled substances.** These conflict with member-school district policies and are banned outright.
- **Performance-contingent pay.** Bonuses tied to stats violate amateur status.
- **Recruiting inducements.** A deal cannot exist as a reason for you to enroll, stay, or transfer.

## Penalties for non-compliance

A student who violates Appendix N may be declared ineligible to compete in GHSA activities. Schools that knowingly permit violations face fines, contest forfeitures, probation, and post-season ineligibility. There is no automatic appeal — restoration of eligibility is at GHSA's discretion.
