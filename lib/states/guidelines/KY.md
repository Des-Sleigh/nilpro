# Kentucky — High School NIL Guidelines

**Status:** Permitted with restrictions
**Last verified:** 2026-04-29
**Primary source:** KHSAA Bylaw 10 (Amateur) and Bylaw 15 (Awards) — [link](https://khsaa.org/common_documents/handbook/2024-2025/2024-25%20Handbook-02-Bylaws.pdf)

## Overview

Kentucky permits high school NIL under updated language added to KHSAA Bylaws 10 and 15 effective with the 2023-2024 handbook. The Kentucky High School Athletic Association (KHSAA) treats NIL compensation as compatible with amateur status as long as the deal does not use school intellectual property, is not a school-driven recruiting inducement, and is paid at a "prevailing market rate" rather than as a hidden athletic-performance bonus.

## Who can participate

Any KHSAA student-athlete may sign NIL deals. There is no class, age, or sport limit. Compensation must come to you as an individual based on your own NIL — not through your team or school.

## Before you sign a deal

- **Read the contract with a parent.** A minor's contract requires parent/guardian co-signature.
- **Verify the rate is market-based.** KHSAA requires that compensation match what someone with similar reach and skills would normally earn — inflated pay can be treated as a hidden athletic bonus.
- **Confirm no school IP is used.** No school name, logo, uniform, mascot, or apparel.
- **Confirm nobody connected to your school is promising the benefit.** Coaches, ADs, boosters, and school employees cannot promise NIL benefits.

## When you sign a deal — your obligations

1. **Notify your athletic director in writing.** KHSAA does not publish a fixed disclosure deadline in Bylaw 10, but written notice before the deal goes live is the safe practice and protects you if questions arise later.
2. **Get parent/guardian co-signature.** Required for minors.
3. **Avoid school and KHSAA intellectual property.** No school name, logo, uniform, mascot, or apparel in your deliverables. No KHSAA marks.
4. **Document the rate.** Be ready to show that the payment matches market rate for the work — not your athletic performance.
5. **Don't accept performance-based pay.** Bonuses tied to stats, points, or wins violate amateur status.
6. **Keep records.** Contract, payments, posts, AD notification, and any market-rate justification.

## Banned deal categories

KHSAA Bylaws 10 and 15 prohibit NIL activity that:

- **Uses any school's name, logo, apparel, uniforms, or other intellectual property.**
- **Uses KHSAA name or trademarks.**
- **Is promised by anyone connected to the school** (coaches, ADs, boosters, foundations).
- **Pays above prevailing market rate.** Inflated pay is treated as a disguised athletic bonus.
- **Pays performance-contingent compensation.** Stats-based bonuses violate amateurism.
- **Conflicts with member-school district policy.** This typically means tobacco, alcohol, cannabis, gambling, weapons, adult entertainment, and illegal substances — confirm with your district.

## Penalties for non-compliance

Violations are handled under KHSAA Bylaw 27, which sets the penalty schedule for eligibility issues. Consequences range from periods of ineligibility to permanent loss of amateur status in that sport. Appeals run through KHSAA Due Process procedures.
